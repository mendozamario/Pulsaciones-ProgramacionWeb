Pulsations.web



This web application has been created with the purpose of registering people and calculating their keystrokes, it must be taken into account that a person's keystrokes are dependent on their gender and sex, therefore there are two types of equations used to calculate this data, once you are registered, you can consult the list of people and their keystrokes.



The equations are the following.

Male.

Age - 220 / 10

Female.

Age - 210 / 10
